coga-helptype

The below example section show how coga-helptype used when coding.

Example: Helptype Using ARIA
<button type="button" coga-extrahelp="uri2 uri3" >undo</button>

URI 2 may read: 

<div id="uri2" coga-helptype="morehelp" coga-hidden="true">
  pressing the undo button will erase all your work on this page.
  Use this button with care.
  If you press it by mistake, press control and y at the same time
  and your answers will come back.
</div>

<a href="functiongethelp()" coga-helptype="humanhelp" coga-hidden="true">
  I want a person to help me</a>
  
<div id="uri3" coga-extrahelp="glossary" coga-hidden="true">
